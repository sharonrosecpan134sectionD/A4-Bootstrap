Event Information

Dates: March 12-14, 2025

Location: O2 Arena london, United kingdom.

Artists: Davido, Asake, Rema, Burna Boy, Wizkid and other performing artists.

Additional Information

Parking

Parking is available at the venue for $10 per day. There are also several nearby parking lots and garages within walking distance.

Food & Beverages

Enjoy a variety of food trucks and stalls offering a wide range of cuisines. Beverages, including alcoholic and non-alcoholic options, will be available throughout the festival grounds.

Accommodation

Several nearby hotels offer special rates for festival attendees. Book early to secure your spot!

Accessibility

The festival grounds are fully accessible with designated viewing areas and facilities. Please contact us in advance for any specific accessibility needs.

Security & Safety

Your safety is our priority. There will be on-site security and first aid stations. Please follow all festival guidelines and report any concerns to festival staff.

Merchandise

Get your festival gear at our merchandise stands. From t-shirts to posters, there's something for everyone to remember the event by.
